Who Are We?

This business was established in 2010 to provide fresh and beautiful floral arrangements that share wonder and excitement to life events. The business recently transitioned from a hybrid model to a totally online store where customers can explore our website and see what the flower shop has to offer. This transition was due to the impact of the Covid-19 Pandemic, the company was forced to close all four of its physical stores including three of its newly opened stores both locally and regionally citing higher operational cost, staffing, marketing and maintenance as its key problems. The business has since then decided to pivot by changing its brick-and-mortar model to a click and mortar business model.

Mission

To provide fresh floral arrangements that share wonder and excitment to life's most cherished events.

Vision

To become the leading and most trusted flower shop in the Caribbean known for fresh flowers, creativity and heartfelt service.
